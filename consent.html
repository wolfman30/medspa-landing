SMS Consent Policy

Last updated: November 7, 2025

Program Overview

AI Wolf Solutions provides missed-call follow-ups, appointment follow-ups, and concierge support via SMS on behalf of participating medical spas. The SMS program is designed to help patients reconnect with clinic staff, reschedule appointments, request callbacks, and receive answers to frequently asked questions.

Opt-In Methods

Patients opt in to SMS messaging when they:

  • Submit a booking or inquiry form that includes SMS consent language.
  • Text START to our concierge number 937-896-2713.
  • Provide verbal consent to clinic staff, who record the approval in our system.

By opting in, patients agree to receive automated text messages related to their inquiries or appointments. Message frequency varies based on engagement but will not exceed four messages per inquiry without additional consent.

Opt-Out Instructions

Patients can opt out at any time by replying STOP. We immediately confirm the opt-out and cancel any pending follow-up messages. Replying START or UNSTOP will restore messaging if the patient wishes to opt back in.

For assistance, patients can reply HELP, call 937-896-2713, or email support@aiwolfsolutions.com.

Message Content

Messages focus on transactional updates such as appointment requests, booking preference collection, and staff follow-up updates. We do not send promotional offers or marketing blasts through this program without prior explicit consent.

Data Usage

We use phone numbers and message content solely to facilitate communication between clinics and their patients. Data is processed in accordance with our privacy policy and is never sold to third parties.

Contact

Questions about this SMS policy can be sent to andrew@aiwolfsolutions.com or by calling 937-896-2713.

How the demo works

We've set up a sandbox clinic that mirrors a real medspa flow. When you call and hang up or text our demo number, our AI receptionist will:

  • Greet you as the demo clinic and offer help with treatments like Botox, fillers, or facials.
  • Gather your full name, desired service, and preferred days or times.
  • Capture preferred times and send a Square sandbox payment link for the holding deposit (generated per demo; it will not charge your card).
  • Share your booking preferences with staff for confirmation (the AI does not finalize appointments).

You can end the conversation at any time by replying STOP or type HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply.

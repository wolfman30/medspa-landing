Responsible messaging and compliance

Clear opt-in

Website visitors opt in through our booking widget checkbox. Incoming callers receive an IVR prompt explaining that a text follow-up will be sent if they consent. All enrollment language includes message frequency, rates, and STOP instructions.

Review the full SMS Consent Policy and our Privacy Policy for the exact terms shared with patients and data practices.

Easy opt-out

Text STOP, END, CANCEL, or UNSUBSCRIBE at any time to halt messages instantly. Our system logs opt-outs and prevents future sends until consent is restored with START or YES.

Verified Toll-Free messaging

We use a verified toll-free number so your clients enjoy reliable deliverability across the United States and Canada. All throughput and campaign approvals are maintained on your behalf.

Audit-ready records

Consent status, message templates, and delivery history are retained for compliance reviews. Administrators can export records at any time.

Why medspa teams choose us

AI concierge, always on

Missed calls trigger intelligent SMS conversations that capture context, surface next-best actions, and loop in your team when a human touch is required.

Guided booking flows

Every clinic gets branded scripts that gather preferred days, times, and full names, then escalate to staff for confirmation - no open tabs required.

Integrated deposits

Collect consultation fees or hold deposits via secure checkout links. We share payment status and booking context with staff; no EMR/EHR or CRM sync in phase 1.

Responsible messaging and compliance

Clear opt-in

Website visitors opt in through our booking widget checkbox. For missed-call follow-ups, we provide consent language and help you implement the right opt-in for your clinic's workflow. All enrollment language includes message frequency, rates, and STOP instructions.

Review the full SMS Consent Policy and our Privacy Policy · Terms & Conditions for the exact terms shared with patients and data practices.

Easy opt-out

Text STOP, END, CANCEL, or UNSUBSCRIBE at any time to halt messages instantly. Our system logs opt-outs and prevents future sends until consent is restored with START or YES.

Keep Your Number for Text Messaging

We keep your phone number and register your brand and use case (campaign) for verified messaging. We handle most of the process; your team only needs to submit the required identification details to begin approval. This entire approval process typically takes 5-7 business days.

Audit-ready records

Consent status, message templates, and delivery history are retained for compliance reviews. Administrators can export records at any time.
